TAX CALCULATOR

Welcome to our Tax Calculator tool! This simple yet powerful calculator helps you estimate your overall income after tax deductions based on your gross annual income, extra income, age group, and applicable deductions.

How It Works

  • Gross Annual Income: Enter your total annual salary before any deductions.
  • Extra Income: Include any additional income sources such as bonuses, commissions, or investment returns.
  • Age Group: Select your age group from the options provided. Different tax rates apply based on your age.
  • Deductions: Add any eligible deductions or exemptions that can be subtracted from your gross income before calculating taxes.

Once you input all the necessary information, the calculator computes your total income after deducting applicable taxes.

Tax Calculation

Taxes are calculated based on your age group:

  • Individuals under 40 years old: Tax rates apply at 30%.
  • Individuals aged between 40 and 60 years: Tax rates apply at 40%.
  • Individuals over 60 years old: Tax rates apply at 10%.

It's important to note that there is no tax levied on incomes below 8 lakhs.
